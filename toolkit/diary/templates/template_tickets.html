Advance Tickets


Tickets for cinema showings are sold from the door approximately 30 minutes before the advertised showing time. You can buy advance tickets on line for selected screenings through www.ticketsource.co.uk (booking fee applies).

Tickets will be available to buy on line until 4 hours before the screening. There will usually be a small number of tickets available first-come-first-served on the door on the night even if the advance allocation is sold out. Early arrival is recommended.
